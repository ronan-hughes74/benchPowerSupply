## About

## Further Comments
This is one of the most important components of the build, as it is on of the few components whose quality has a vital impact on the quality of the final product. For example, in most cases the only difference between a cheap and expensive switch is the quality of the user experience. A nicer click, a heavier feel, a better installation, etc. However no matter what kind of switch you get, it will likely still be fully capable of its on/off functions, because it is a fundementally simple task to accomplish. The buck module needs to be able to accurately step up and down the voltage. This is a much more complicated function, and the quality of the device will affect its ability to achieve it. So does it concern me that it was only $1.00 off Ali express? Yes, a little bit. However with proper testing, I'll be able to decide to keep this one, or purchase a new one.
